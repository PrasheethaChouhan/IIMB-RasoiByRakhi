Our Story

Passion for Authentic Flavors

Rasoi by Rakhi began with a simple mission: to bring the authentic taste of home-cooked meals to busy professionals and families.

Chef Rakhi, with over 15 years of culinary expertise, crafts each dish with handpicked ingredients and traditional recipes passed down through generations.

What started as a small home kitchen has now evolved into a beloved cloud kitchen, serving hundreds of satisfied customers daily while maintaining the same love and care in every dish.

Frequently Asked Questions

Find answers to common questions about our services

What are your operating hours?

Rasoi by Rakhi is open 7 days a week from 11:00 AM to 10:00 PM. We accept orders throughout these hours, but please note that during peak hours (12:00 PM - 2:00 PM and 7:00 PM - 9:00 PM), delivery times may be slightly longer.

What areas do you deliver to?

We currently deliver to all areas in Pune. For locations outside our delivery zone, we partner with third-party delivery services which may have additional delivery charges. Please check our Delivery Information section for more details on specific areas and delivery charges.

How long does delivery usually take?

Our standard delivery time is 30-45 minutes, depending on your location and current order volume. During peak hours or adverse weather conditions, delivery may take slightly longer. You can track your order status through our app or by calling our customer service number.

Do you cater for special dietary requirements?

Yes, we offer a variety of options for different dietary needs including vegetarian, vegan, gluten-free, and low-calorie meals. Please mention your specific requirements in the "Special Instructions" section when placing your order, or call us directly to discuss your needs.

What payment methods do you accept?

We accept various payment methods including credit/debit cards, UPI, digital wallets (Paytm, Google Pay, PhonePe), and cash on delivery. For corporate orders and catering services, we also offer the option of bank transfers. Visit our Payment Options section for more details.

How can I provide feedback about my order?

We value your feedback! You can rate your order and provide comments through our app or website after delivery. Alternatively, you can email us at theprasheeta@gmail.com or call our customer service number. Your feedback helps us improve our services and maintain the quality of our food.

Delivery Information

Everything you need to know about our delivery service

Our Delivery Process

At Rasoi by Rakhi, we take pride in delivering fresh, hot meals directly to your doorstep. Our delivery process is designed to ensure that your food arrives in perfect condition, maintaining the same quality as if you were dining in our kitchen.

Once your order is confirmed, our chefs prepare your meal with the freshest ingredients. The food is then carefully packaged in eco-friendly containers that maintain temperature and prevent spillage. Our dedicated delivery team ensures that your order reaches you within the promised time frame.

Delivery Timing

We deliver from 11:00 AM to 10:00 PM, seven days a week. Delivery times may vary based on:

  • Distance: Delivery to Zone A areas typically takes 30-40 minutes, Zone B takes 40-50 minutes, and Zone C takes 50-60 minutes.
  • Weather Conditions: During monsoon or adverse weather, delivery might take longer than usual.
  • Order Volume: During peak hours (lunch and dinner times), please allow for additional delivery time.

Payment Options

Convenient and secure ways to pay for your order

Cash on Delivery

Pay with cash when your order arrives at your doorstep. Our delivery personnel carry change, but we appreciate it if you can provide the exact amount.

Credit/Debit Cards

We accept all major credit and debit cards including Visa, MasterCard, American Express, and RuPay. Card payments can be made online or through our POS machine at the time of delivery.

UPI Payments

Make instant transfers using any UPI app including Google Pay, PhonePe, Paytm, and BHIM. Scan our QR code or use our UPI ID for quick and easy payments.

Digital Wallets

We support payments through popular digital wallets for a seamless checkout experience. Link your preferred payment method to your wallet for faster transactions.

Secure Payments

All online transactions on our platform are secured with industry-standard encryption protocols. We do not store your card details or share your payment information with third parties.

Our payment gateway is PCI DSS compliant, ensuring that your sensitive data is handled with the highest security standards.
